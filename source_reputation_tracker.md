## How to Read This Tracker

### Source credibility trend indicators
- **↑ Improving** — Recent 3-score avg >0.5 above overall avg (≥3 appearances). Apply +0.3 to all subscribers.
- **↓ Declining** — Recent 3-score avg >0.5 below overall avg (≥3 appearances). Apply -0.5 to all subscribers.
- **⚠ Volatile** — Std dev >1.5 across all appearances (≥3 appearances). Flag in newsletter, no adjustment.
- **→ Stable** — Consistent scoring. No adjustment.
- **🆕 New** — Fewer than 3 appearances. No adjustment.

### Credibility ratings
- **Very High (9.0–10.0):** Rigorous primary sourcing, accurate representation, original reporting
- **High (7.5–8.9):** Generally well-sourced, minor gaps
- **Moderate (6.0–7.4):** Mix of original and secondary sourcing, occasional accuracy issues
- **Low (4.0–5.9):** Primarily aggregation, weak sourcing
- **Flagged (<4.0):** Repeated misrepresentation — maximum scrutiny

### Relevance weight rules (per subscriber — applies to both sub-tags and sources)
- **Active positive:** +0.2 per confirmation, max **+1.5**
- **Active negative:** -0.2 per confirmation, min **-1.5**
- **Passive decay:** ±0.2 toward 0 each appearance without confirmation (positive weight → -0.2, negative weight → +0.2)
- **14-day decay:** additional ±0.2 toward 0 if no confirming feedback in 14 days
- **Decay never overshoots 0** — floor and ceiling are -1.5 and +1.5
- **Effect on score:** direct addition/subtraction to article's relevance component
- **Combined cap:** total sub-tag + source relevance adjustment capped at ±1.5 per article per subscriber
